Some UK water wholesalers provide service updates and alerts on their websites and social media pages. Where information is available, we have provided a link to updates here. Please select your Wholesaler from the list below.

Emergencies:

You can report an emergency, disruption to your water supply, water quality or wastewater service to Castle Water during office hours by calling 01250 718700. Outside normal office hours, in an emergency, or if you experience unplanned changes or disruption to your water supply, water quality or wastewater service, please call the emergency contact number for your local wholesaler. This emergency service is available 24 / 7. List of wholesaler emergency numbers.
